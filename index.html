Meditation

Meditation is a powerful tool that can be used to train your attention and awareness to achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state. It is practiced in many religions and cultures across the globe. There are many variations in meditation techniques. In its most basic form it is simply focusing ones attention inward, letting external distractions and thoughts come and go naturally, observing without placing judgement.
