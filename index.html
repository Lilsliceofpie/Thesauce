Self-improvement techniques

Meditation

Meditation is a powerful tool that can be used to train your attention and awareness to achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state. It is practiced by many religions and cultures across the globe. There are many variations in meditation techniques. In its most basic form it is simply focusing ones attention inward, letting external distractions and thoughts come and go naturally, observing without placing judgement. Here you will find an instructional video to get you started.

Breathwork

Breathwork is a term that encompasses a wide range of breathing exercises in which conscious control of the breath is used to influence a persons physical, emotional or mental state. Breathwork goes hand in hand with other theraputic techniques such as meditation. It has its roots in yoga and has been practiced in some parts of the world for thousands of years. Recently it has become more established in the mainstream, popularized by proponents such as Wim Hof. Here you will find an instructional video, simply breath along.

Calesthenics

Calesthenics is a form of strength training that uses compound movements to exercise large muscle groups, usually only using bodyweight or with minimal equiptment. It is intended to increase strength and flexibility, improving posture while reducing the risk of developing muscle imbalances. There are records of the practice being used as far back as ancient Greece and China. Here you will find an instructional video to get you started.
